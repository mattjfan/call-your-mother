Call Your Mother is designed as an easy to use utility app to help you stay in touch with friends, family, and other important contacts. Our app lets you choose which contacts you want to receive reminders for, and how frequently, and passively tracks calling behavior outside of the app using android call logs to accurately send reminders to call a contact after the chosen period has elapsed without calling them.

External libraries:

  • ShapeTextDrawable: used to create a Drawable of initials in the case that a contact does not have a picture
  • Google Gson: a JSON parsing library used to format the stored data
  • AndroidX CardView: the CardView container used in the HomeFragment and UserFragment
  • Navigation Component: used to create a Navigation graph for easier transitions between fragments
  • Safe Args: used to pass data between different fragments and activities in a safe and easy way
